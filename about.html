About Sills Cummis & Gross

Sills Cummis & Gross is a commercial law firm dedicated to serving businesses at every stage of growth. With offices in Newark, New York, and Florida, we provide strategic legal counsel to companies ranging from emerging startups to established enterprises.

For decades, our attorneys have built a reputation for business-focused advice, efficient case management, and deep industry expertise. We understand that your legal challenges are also business challenges, and we approach each matter with that perspective.

Our commitment is to deliver practical, sophisticated legal solutions that align with your business goals and budget.
